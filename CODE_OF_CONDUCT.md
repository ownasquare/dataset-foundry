# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We commit to making participation in Dataset Foundry a welcoming, harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact directly.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and product outcomes.
- Protect private training data, credentials, and security reports.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and community boundaries.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption, doxxing, and sharing
another person's private data are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately using the contact method listed on the maintainer's GitHub profile.
Do not post sensitive conduct reports in a public issue. Maintainers may edit or remove content and
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical, with conflicts of interest disclosed and avoided.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.

This policy is based on the principles of the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
